Technology has become a huge part of our lives. The majority of internet users admit to using technology for more than two hours at a time. As the consumption of technology increases, so do the long-term permanent health implications associated with it.

Digital eye strain has become a common issue among frequent internet users and can lead to permanent damage to your eyes. The Digital Eye Strain Prevention Tool aims to reduce the spread of the problem by implementing the 20-20-20 rule.
